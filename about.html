Who We Are

Resilient North Uganda (RNU) is a fully registered Community-Based Organization (CBO) working with smallholder farmers, women, youth, and post-conflict communities in Northern and Eastern Uganda.

Our Vision

Resilient, prosperous, and food-secure rural communities sustainably managing their natural resources.

Our Mission

To empower smallholder farmers, women, and youth through climate-smart agriculture, livelihood diversification, environmental conservation, and inclusive finance.

Core Focus Areas

  • Climate-Smart & Market-Oriented Agriculture
  • Livelihood Diversification & Income Generation
  • Village Savings & Financial Inclusion
  • Women & Youth Economic Empowerment
  • Climate Adaptation & Environmental Protection
  • Monitoring, Evaluation & Learning (MEL)
