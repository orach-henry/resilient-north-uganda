Who We Are

Resilient North Uganda (RNU) is a fully registered Community-Based Organization (CBO) working with smallholder farmers, women, youth, and post-conflict communities in Northern Uganda. We focus on strengthening climate resilience, food security, sustainable livelihoods, and inclusive rural development.

RNU operates at the intersection of climate-smart agriculture, livelihood diversification, community finance, and institutional capacity building, ensuring that development interventions are locally grounded and environmentally sustainable.

Our Vision

Resilient, prosperous, and food-secure communities in Northern Uganda living in harmony with their environment.

Our Mission

To empower vulnerable communities through climate-resilient agriculture, diversified livelihoods, inclusive finance, and strong local institutions.

Our Operating Context

Northern Uganda is emerging from decades of conflict, climate shocks, and economic exclusion. Communities face declining soil fertility, erratic rainfall, limited access to finance, and weak market linkages.

RNU was founded to address these interconnected challenges by supporting locally driven solutions that strengthen resilience while creating sustainable economic opportunities.
