Who We Are

Resilient North Uganda (RNU) is a fully registered Community-Based Organization (CBO) working with smallholder farmers, women, youth, and post-conflict communities in Northern Uganda.

We strengthen climate resilience, food security, sustainable livelihoods, and inclusive rural development through locally driven and environmentally sustainable interventions.

Our Vision

Resilient, prosperous, and food-secure rural communities living in harmony with their environment.

Our Mission

To empower vulnerable communities through climate-smart agriculture, diversified livelihoods, inclusive finance, and strong local institutions.
