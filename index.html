Meet Our Founder

Henry Orach – PhD Scholar in Rural and Regional Development
Sichuan Agricultural University | Uganda

Henry Orach is a dedicated scholar and rural development advocate pursuing his PhD in Rural and Regional Development at Sichuan Agricultural University, China. Originally from Namokora, a small village in northern Uganda, Henry’s life journey has been shaped by deep firsthand experiences of the challenges faced by rural farming communities.

Driven by both passion and purpose, Henry earned a bachelor's degree in economics through a scholarship program and later completed a master's in regional economics. His research explores the intersections of agricultural productivity, income diversification, access to credit, and infrastructure development, with a focus on post-conflict northern Uganda.

He believes that success in development is best measured by visible improvements in people's lives: food security, health outcomes, better roads, rising income, and financial access.
