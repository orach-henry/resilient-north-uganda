Who We Are

Resilient North Uganda (RNU) is a fully registered Community-Based Organization working to strengthen climate resilience, sustainable agriculture, livelihoods, and inclusive economic development among smallholder farmers and rural households in Uganda.

What We Do

Climate-Smart Agriculture

Improving productivity and resilience through sustainable farming systems.

Livelihoods & Income

Supporting off-farm enterprises and financial inclusion.

Environment & Climate

Land restoration, clean energy, and natural resource management.

Capacity Building

Extension services, training, and farmer organization strengthening.

Our Commitment

RNU works closely with communities, local governments, farmer associations, and development partners to deliver evidence-based, inclusive, and sustainable solutions aligned with national and global development priorities.
