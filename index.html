About Our NGO

Resilient North Uganda is committed to rural transformation in Northern Uganda through climate-smart agriculture, diversified livelihoods, and community savings initiatives.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission: To empower rural communities in Northern Uganda through sustainable agriculture, inclusive livelihoods, and financial resilience.

Vision: A hunger-free, self-reliant Northern Uganda where communities thrive despite climate and economic challenges.
