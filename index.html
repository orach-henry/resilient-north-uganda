About Us

Resilient North Uganda is a community-rooted NGO focused on transforming lives through agriculture, inclusive livelihoods, and rural finance. We are guided by local knowledge, post-war resilience, and a vision of sustainable prosperity for all.

Our Vision

A hunger-free, self-reliant Northern Uganda where communities thrive despite climate and economic challenges.

Our Mission

To empower rural communities in Northern Uganda through sustainable agriculture, inclusive livelihoods, and financial resilience.
