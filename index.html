About Our NGO

Mission:
To empower rural communities in Northern Uganda through sustainable agriculture, inclusive livelihoods, and financial resilience.
Vision:
A hunger-free, self-reliant Northern Uganda where communities thrive despite climate and economic challenges.

Meet Our Founder

Henry Orach – PhD Scholar in Rural and Regional Development
Sichuan Agricultural University | Uganda

Henry Orach is a dedicated scholar in Rural and Regional Development pursuing his PhD in regional economic transformation through a scholarship program. His research focuses on identifying the key growth drivers for rural prosperity: improved agriculture, income diversification, access to credit, and infrastructure development—especially in the post-conflict context of Northern Uganda.

He believes success is seen in real outcomes: more food on the table, better health, stronger roads, and access to financial services.
